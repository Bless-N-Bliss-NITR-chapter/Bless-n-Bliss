Seminar at NUS Business School

Singapore

10th October 2019

Work for a good change together can bring a new change in the society. To bring a massive change in society Cyclothon 3.0 started by our Founder, Chairman Akram Ansari Recently Akram sir in collaboration with AIESEC Singapore organized a seminar at NUS Business School, National University of Singapore There Akram sir enlightened everyone about the moto and goal of Cyclothon 3.0. A great interactive session was organized there among the students and Akram sir. The enthusiasm and delight of those students to bring change in the society was remarkable. Our heartfelt gratitude to AISEC Singapore for organizing the seminar. Special Thanks to Jamie Teo Phoeba Tan Yi Xiu, Yuxin Yip, Saurabh Kumar for the help.

Cyclothon is a awareness campaign through long Cycle Journey started by our founder & Chairman Md. Akram Ansari.

Cyclothon 1.0: Our founder started a 100 kilometers Cycle Journey from Bhubaneswar to Jajpur Road on 15th August 2017 to create awareness on Gender equality.

Cyclothon 2.0: Akram Sir travelled around 400 kilometers from Jajpur road to Ranchi to create awareness of our Project Sikshalaya(Right to Education).

Cyclothon 3.0: We successfully organized the flag off ceremony with lots and lots of support from all of you. Our chief guest hon'ble IG of training Smt. Priya Dubey was present there to inagurate the flag off ceremony. With all anticipation and excitement we started off our journey Cyclothon 3.0. Thanks hon'ble IG mam for all your love and support.

The mission to glory is on a plane to board soon. Our founder and chairman Akram Ansari set his feet to start a journey of 6000 kms in 60 days covering 6 nations on his bicycle.We shall be sermonizing and creating a sea of awareness education of under privileged children, women safety and employment of transgenders through seminars , such that a glimpse of hope propagates.

Creating a better world requires teamwork, patnerships and collaboration, as we need an entire army of organizations to work together to build a better world within the next few decades.This means organizations must Embrace their benefits of cooperating with one another.Similarly Our Collaboration will lead our missions to new heights of achievements.

We are doing seminars regarding awareness program for unprivilaged children, employment of transgenders in 6 countries all over the world such as India, Bangladesh, Myamar, Thailand, Malasiya, Singapore on eve of Cyclothon 3.0.The whole team of Bless N Bliss Foundation is full of gratitude towords all the seminar partners for supporting us in Cyclothon 3.0.
