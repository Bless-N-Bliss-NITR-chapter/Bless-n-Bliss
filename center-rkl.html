OUR PROJECTS

SAMARTHAN

One of the primary focuses of Bless N Bliss Foundation has been towards the education and imparting knowledge to the underprivileged children, and we don't lose sight of that. Through Sikshalaya, we directly get that help to the kids, but there are many other institutions which are already doing the same. But some of them lack certain resources and could use some help in continuing the noble deeds. That is why the Samarthan Project was initiated; in order to collaborate with the financially weak institutions, which also help teach the underprivileged children.

Through Samarthan, Bless N Bliss Foundation either sponsors their education or helps provide them with basic amenities such as seating arrangements, stationary items, and water, electricity and sanitation facilities. Samarthan has already been successful in Rourkela, Ranchi and Jajpur Road. We do all we can to help the cause, and with your help we can do more.

SHIKHYALAYA

Education is one of the most wonderful gifts you can give to a child, but many children in this country don’t get that. Believing this, Bn’B established its Sikshalaya project in order to help educate the underprivileged children. Education Centres have been established in cities like Rourkela and Ranchi. The education centres help provide the children with books, notes, tutors, and guardians to guide them through their educational journey, which every child deserves. They ensure to enroll every child in school for them to get primary education and tutors to help them further.

Besides this, there are many local volunteers who visit these centres, to bring the children books, board games, and even stay hours to help them study. Shikshalaya was one of the first projects undertaken by Bless N Bliss Foundation, to not just educate them but with the motive to give these kids a sense of responsibility and authority in their lives, so that they overcome the prevailing atrocities, and realize their dreams. Bless N Bliss Foundation will continue to help them in their journey.

SAKSHAM

Bless N Bliss Foundation Rourkela, club of NIT Rourkela is Registered in literary society under Student Activity Centre. Saksham focuses on providing mental and physical training to the girls of schools and colleges so that they can protect themselves in the time of need. BnB approached schools and colleges in Rourkela and organized self-defense sessions for girls, taught by certified trainers.

JAGRUKTA

"Project Jagrukta" is a Small initiative towards breaking the social stigma associated with menstruation and creating awareness about female hygiene and monthly cycle. Menstrual hygiene is vital for empowerment and wellbeing of women worldwide. Apart from mere access to sanitary napkins and appropriate toilets, it is also about ensuring the fact that women live in an environment that values and support their ability to manage menstruation with dignity.

From October of 2020, we have selected “Jagda Orphanage” having around 15 girls as the workplace from and providing them with “sanitary napkins”. We will look after their monthly requirements till December of 2021. NITR Dean Academics Prof Saroj Kumar Patel, President of Student Activity Center Prof. Seemita Mohanty, Club Faculty Advisor Prof. Uttam Kumar Mishra & Vice President of Literary Society Dr. Upendra Gundala have supported a lot to make this project a successful one.
