RANCHI

BnB Ranchi

Bless'n'Bliss started it's journey in Ranchi on 25th December 2016

One of the prominent centres of Bless'n'Bliss is placed at the city of Ranchi.

Our Main Workplace in Ranchi

  • Education Centre -1(EC-1)

Under the Project Sikshalaya we have provided regular education to the unprivileged students in our workplaces. We organize regular study tours for the students.

BnB Ranchi members have contributed the most to our Flagship event Cyclothon 3.0.

BnB Ranchi has organized 2 blood donation camps for the betterment of the Society.

Amidst the coronavirus pandemic, Bless'n'Bliss has taken a step forward to provide essential Ration items to the needy in Ranchi through ration distribution drive

OUR COLLABORATIONS

BnB Ranchi has collaborated with DAV SWARNREKHA Public School for books donation drive.

In the Pandemic period, Bless'n'Bliss has collaborated with NIFFT Ranchi to do a ration distribution drive. We have distributed 110 packets of groceries at Nirmala Kusht Colony, Education Centre(EC-1) of Bless'n'Bliss, Ranchi.

OUR PROJECTS

SIKSHALAYA

BnB Shikshalaya in Ranchi comprises of education centers that impart knowledge to the underprivileged children and help them realize their dreams. Our motive is to give these kids a sense of responsibility and authority on their lives, so that they overcome the prevailing atrocities.

SAMARTHAN

BnB Samarthan in Ranchi focuses on collaboration with financially weak institutions, which impart knowledge to the underprivileged children, by either sponsoring their education or providing them with basic amenities su ch as seating arrangements, stationery items, and water, electricity and sanitation facilities.
