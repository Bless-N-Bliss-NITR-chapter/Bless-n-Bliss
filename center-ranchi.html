SAMARTHAN

BnB Samarthan focuses on collaboration with financially weak institutions, which impart knowledge to the underprivileged children, by either sponsoring their education or providing them with basic amenities such as seating arrangements, stationery items, and water, electricity and sanitation facilities.

SHIKSHALAYA

BnB Shikshalaya comprises of education centers that impart knowledge to the underprivileged children and help them realize their dreams. Our motive is to give these kids a sense of responsibility and authority on their lives, so that they overcome the prevailing atrocities.

SAKSHAM

"Saksham" is an initiative to provide self-defence training classes for girls. Through this project, Experts teach self defence techniques to the girls.This project is to make girls conquer their fear of the current problems of the Society. We are always encouraged by Authorities of the Institute to organize such activities for taking a step forward in making women self-sufficient.
