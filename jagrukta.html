JAGRUKTA

"Project Jagrukta" is a Small initiative towards breaking the social stigma associated with menstruation and creating awareness about female hygiene and monthly cycle. Menstrual hygiene is vital for empowerment and wellbeing of women worldwide. Apart from mere access to sanitary napkins and appropriate toilets, it is also about ensuring the fact that women live in an environment that values and support their ability to manage menstruation with dignity.

Rourkela

Volunteers: 45

Project Jagrukta is mainly for orphanage girls who have lack of proper guidance regarding their health and personal hygiene and at the time of teenage they feel shy to tell their health problems to their female teachers also.So, under this project, the members of BlessNBliss are going to select some orphanages nearby institute premises in Rourkela as our workplace. Then our team of girls will make sure to provide one to one support to every growing girl with advices regarding female hygiene and importance of menstrual cycle in female body with the help of PPTs, demonstration and QA sessions.

From October of 2020, we have selected “Jagda Orphanage” having around 15 girls as the workplace from and providing them with “sanitary napkins”. We will look after their monthly requirements till December of 2021. NITR Dean Academics Prof Saroj Kumar Patel, President of Student Activity Center Prof. Seemita Mohanty, Club Faculty Advisor Prof. Uttam Kumar Mishra & Vice President of Literary Society Dr. Upendra Gundala have supported a lot to make this project a successful one.

We are planning to conduct workshop regarding menstrual hygiene awareness and expand this project across the orphanages and schools after the corona pandemic period."Even access to orphanages directly as a constraint, giving suggestions and advices to the girls regarding personal hygiene through telephonic communication" :-this work mode of team gives a positive hope that this will create a positive impact on at least small marginal section of underprivileged orphanage girls.
